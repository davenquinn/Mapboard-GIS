# Basemaps

<Figure
  src="/docs/basemaps/tile-layer-selector.png"
  width={280}
  className="right"
>
  Tile layer selector
</Figure>

**Mapboard GIS** includes a wide variety of [Mapbox basemaps](https://www.mapbox.com/maps)
to contextualize mapping.
Additionally, `mbtiles`-formatted basemaps and streaming imagery can be added,
allowing a wide range of map imagery to be used in the application.

:::note
Caching of tiles from Mapbox's servers will be supported a later version of the app.
:::

<Figure src="/docs/basemaps/new-tile-layer.png" width={280} className="right">
  The form for adding a new tile layer to the map
</Figure>
